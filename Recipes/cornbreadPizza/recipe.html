Cornbread Crust Pizza

Author: Peyton Nelson


Makes for a delicious and unique pizza. This recipe takes the classic idea of a pizza but gives it a subtle yet great tasting twist.

Cornbread Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup corn meal
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 2 eggs

Honey Butter Ingredients

  • 1 stick butter
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)

Pizza Ingredients

  • Your Favorite Pizza Toppings

Recommendations

  • pizza sauce
  • mozzarella cheese
  • pepperoni
  • parmesan (to sprinkle on top)
  • italian seasoning
  • jalapeños
  • red bell pepper
  • onion

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F
  2. Mix all dry cornbread ingredients in a large bowl
  3. Add the wet ingredients to the large bowl and mix together
  4. Prepare a pizza pan by lining it with tin foil, then grease it and lightly dust with cornmeal
  5. Spread the cornbread out on the pan
  6. Bake in the oven for 10 minutes
  7. Remove from the oven
  8. Drizzle a bit of the honey butter on the cornbread and let soak in
  9. Add your favorite pizza toppings
  10. Place back in the oven for an additional 12-15 minutes
  11. Enjoy! (Serve with honey butter to drizzle on top)
